Research Program

Research Statement

Recent work focuses on the Structural Information Evolution framework and related studies in emergent causality, spectral-dimension flow, and fractal geometric structures. These investigations seek to establish mathematically consistent links between information, entropy, geometry, and physical observables across scales.
